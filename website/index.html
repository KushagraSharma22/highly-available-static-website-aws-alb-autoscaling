Why 3-Tier

Built for resilience, designed to scale

Every layer of this stack — presentation, application, and data — is decoupled, redundant, and independently scalable, following the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

Presentation Tier

Route 53 and an Application Load Balancer route traffic across zones, absorbing spikes without a single point of failure.

Application Tier

An Auto Scaling Group of EC2 instances stretches and shrinks with demand across two Availability Zones.

Data Tier

Amazon RDS provides a managed, Multi-AZ relational database with automated backups and failover.

Observability

CloudWatch and SNS keep watch around the clock, alerting the team the moment a metric drifts.
